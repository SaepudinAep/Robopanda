About Robopanda

Robopanda is a learning initiative focused on robotics and coding, designed to build strong logical thinking, structured problem-solving, and fundamental understanding of technology.

Learning Focus

  • Programming logic fundamentals
  • Visual and text-based coding
  • Beginner robotics and IoT
  • Problem solving & computational thinking

Roadmap

Learning materials will be released gradually, with a minimum of one new topic per week. This page serves as the foundation before expanding into a full learning platform.
